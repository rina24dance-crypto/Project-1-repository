My story from America

Today I want to tell you a story when I was in America in 2023. It was in the airport of DC when I was having my first flight there. I was really tired and just wanted to rest that night but I missed the DC flight to Chicago because of a delay so I decided to find a hotel nearby.

But it was hard to do. I didn't even know how to call a cab. I didn't have the internet and the right charger for my phone.
So I went downstairs to find the taxi in the airport and I did it despite being feared and exhausted.

Then one of the SCARIEST MOMENTS took place. I got to the hotel but then had to return to the airport by step.

it's a long story actually but I want to put stress on the fact that YOU GOTTA STAY INSIDE AT NIGHT IF YOU JUST ARRIVED AT THE PLACE YOU HAVE NEVER BEEN TO.

I ended up well but what I went through is gonna stay with me forever.
